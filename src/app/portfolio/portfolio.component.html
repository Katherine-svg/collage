Katherine is a digital artist whose work enmulates early dada and futurism movements.

Golden eye

Gustav Klimt inspired this series. His abstract shapes around his human figures look very balanced and dreamy. I tried to emulate this facade as much as possible without becoming him. I brought some looseness and modernity with the stock photographs I pulled from prexel.

Have to be asleep to dream

Sun rises early in the morning when the skies are dark blue above. The stars are still visible. It has a layer of a galaxy far from us all. Skies are evaporated water and she is under the water. She is surrounded by glow in a dreamy state. Dreamy sharp organic lines. Glowing on her own.

Not anymore

Photographs in lower orbit with momentaneous Snaps of life. Manually cut jagged geometric shapes. Buildings soar into the sky. They point to an airplane that's flying towards its destination. Urban picture. A collage of everyday life.

Rising buildings

Rising buildings towards a black moon. A quarter of a moon blazing above in the pink or green or purple skies. Evaporated water sky divided in rectangle shapes in creamy colors. Like ice cream. Dream catcher catches a dream where the blazing sun is. Awake the dreamer.

Pink lips

Blue overwhelms the picture but pink is bright enough on a small amount. Lips as red as the dying flowers on top of books. Overshadowed by lips are old flowers under pink shadow. In this collage, shadows contradict themselves. I let lots of white space to leave a little breathing room. The yellow flowers do nothing but just sit there.
